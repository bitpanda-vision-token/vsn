  • BEST and Pantos were built with distinct, valuable purposesBEST acted as the key to our loyalty programme, while Pantos focused on interoperability across blockchains, one of Web3s biggest challenges. Over time, it became clear that their strengths couldand shouldbe brought together.

    The merge into Vision is more than a consolidation. Its a strategic reset. By unifying both tokens into one, were simplifying the token landscape, removing long-standing limitations, and finally enabling a token thats ready to grow beyond Bitpandas borders. Vision is built to operate across platforms, protocols, and ecosystemswith the flexibility, governance, and utility to thrive in a fully onchain future. This opens the door to global exchange listings and decentralised liquidity.

    We believe Web3 is the next generation of the internetopen, decentralised, and user-owned. Vision is our answer to that future. With a clean foundation and true onchain governance, it will open the door to new partnerships, integrations, and real-world use cases across established Web3 ecosystems. This is more than a token merge. Its the start of something much bigger.

    • Holders will be able to convert their tokens at the following fixed rates:

  • Should you not want to accept the merge, you are welcome to take your BEST and/or PAN to an external wallet. Bear in mind, however, that we will not offer any kind of trading markets for either BEST or PAN after July 16 10:00 CEST. All BEST and PAN that remain on Bitpanda will be automatically converted one year after the initial merge event.

  • The Vision Loyalty Programme moves beyond rigid VIP tiers. Benefits like voting power and Spotlight rewards now scale smoothly with your engagementthe more you hold/stake, the more you unlock.

  • All current BEST VIPs will keep their increased staking rewards and boosted Bitpanda Cash Plus yields based on their VIP level at the time the merge date is announced until the end of 2025, no matter how much they hold.

    From 2026 onward, youll be able to continue enjoying these benefits through the Bitpanda Club.

    Please note: After the merge date is announced, it will no longer be possible to unlock new BEST VIP levels. Also, with the launch of the Vision loyalty programme, the BEST Weekly Rewards and Instant Trade Bonus will be phased out making room for staking rewards and bringing back trade fee discounts.

  • Yes and the good news is: BEST VIP4 and VIP5 members at the time the merge date is announced will retain their Bitpanda Club status until the end of 2025, regardless of their current holdings. So theres no need to worry your status is secure for now. It will however no longer possible to enter the Club or upgrade your status through BEST holdings, once the merge date has been announced. Starting in 2026, VSN holdings will no longer grant automatic access to the Club however, youll still be able to qualify through trading activity, as always.

  • For Austrian users the merge will not be a tax event. We will track the merged assets for tax withholding to ensure they are taxed properly in the future. Based on the Austrian Revenue the merge is treated along the lines of a crypto-to-crypto swap. That means that for BEST and PAN that were bought starting 1 March 2021 the existing tax base will be carried over to VSN and will be applied for tax withholding in a future sell transaction. For BEST and PAN that were bought before 1 March 2021 the BEST and PAN price as of the swap will be locked in. In a future sell transaction VSN will be treated as new assets for tax withholding purposes.

    Users who are tax resident in other countries have to check the applicable local tax regulations and report their taxes accordingly.
